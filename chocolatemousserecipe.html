Chocolate mousse


Ingredients:

  1. 200g dark chocolate
  2. 4 eggs (separated)
  3. 1/4 cup granulated sugar
  4. 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  5. pinch of salt
  6. whipped cream and chocolate shavings (optional)

Step-by-step instructions:

    Step 1

    Melt the chopped dark chocolate in a heatproof bowl over shimmering water,stirring until smooth. Remove from heat and let it cool slightly.

    Step 2

    In a separate bowl, whisk the egg yolks, sugar, vanilla extract, and salt until pale and creamy.

    Step 3

    Gradually pour the melted chocolate into the egg yolk mixture, stirring until well combined.

    Step 4

    In another clean bowl, beat the egg whites until stiff peaks form.

    Step 5

    Gently fold the beaten egg whites into the chocolate mixture until no streaks remain.

    Step 6

    Divide the mousse mixture into serving glasses or bowls. Cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours, or until set.

    Step 7

    Before serving, you can top the chocolate mousse with whipped cream and chocolate shavings for an extra touch of indulgence.
